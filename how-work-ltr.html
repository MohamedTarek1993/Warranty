How It Works

We at The Warranty Company firmly believe in accessibility, transparency and credibility to enable a better ecosystem and drive understanding to a must have service, below is a brief explanation on the process and why should you consider us as your product protection partner.

Paid money but you have changed your mind? Or by a miracle you have found a better package or a better rate? Effective immediately

You are by default enrolled and eligible after your purchase, your extended warranty starts right after the end of the manufacturer warranty and if you have opted in accidental damage from handling; it is effective from date of purchase.

Wide range of covers

You are still with us? We are excited and we are sure you are as excited as we are. Now you can enjoy doing what you do and let us worry about the safety and service of your products, why are we confident that we can take up the challenge you may ask. We offer a wide range of cover for all products and for nearly all prices so we can be your one stop shop for all your warranty and repair needs.

Single point of contact

Single point of contact, once you connect with us in any convenient mean whether through chat, email or phone, we will handle the rest from there after asking you few basic questions to validate the plan you have purchased.

Repair & Replacement at anywhere

We will ensure repairing your product, should we fail to do so then we will replace it instantly. We can collect your product from wherever you may be, even if you were at the other end of the world, as long as you have a registered address.

In addition to manufacturer warranty

Worried you will lose your manufacturer warranty? on the contrary, any existing manufacturer warranty on your product will remain intact
